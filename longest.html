The 10 Longest Coaster Tracks Ever

10. Desperado - 5,843 feet. This roller coaster literally begins in the middle of a casino and goes through the roof! Complete With a 225 foot drop into an underground tunnel, this is one you will not want to miss. Locted in Bill's Casino in Nevada, United States.

9. Incrediocoaster - 6,072 feet. Disney California Adventure pulled off a coaster that looks like a wooden coaster, but is actually a magnetic launched coasater that had a dramatic loop and goes from 0 to 5 mph in just 4 seconds. Formerly called "California Screamin.'"

8. Voyage - 6,442 feet. Frequently voted near the top of wooden coaster lists, this coaster has a height of 163 feet, a speed of 67.4 mph, several underground tunnels, and the most airtime of any wood coaster (24.2 seconds!) Located at Holiday World in Indiana, United States.

7. Formula Rossa - 6,562 feet. This intensely fast coaster needs a lot of track to dissipate its insanely fast top speed of 149 mph. Features a launch that goes 0-62 in 2 seconds! Located in Abu Dhabi, United Arab Emirates.

6. Millenium Force - 6,595 feet. Only takes 2:20 to cover the entire track length, since it has a speed of 93 mph. At it's tallest point, this coaster rises 310 feet in the air. A classic of Cedar Point, Ohio.

5. Fury 325 - 6,602 feet. Not only is this coaster long, it it incredibly tall - 325 feet. Arguably one of the most popular giga-coasters on the east coast. Located in Carowinds, North Carolina.

4. Fujiyama - 6,709 feet. Also happens to be among the tallest and fastest coasters (259 feet, and 80.8 mph, respectively). A magnificent steel hypercoaster located in Fuji-Q Highland amusement park in Yamanashi, Japan.

3. The Beast - 7,359 feet. So long it actually requires two lift hills! Among the fastest wooden roller coasters too. Races through the Ohio woods in Kings Island.

2. The Ultimate - 7,442 feet. May hold the record for longest duration, at 5:36 minutes. Like the beast, The Ultimate features two lift hills. Courtesy of Lightwater Valley, United Kingdom.

1. Steel Dragon 2000 - 8,133 feet. Only roller coaster in the world to cross 8,000 feet. Rises a towering 318 feet and goes all the way to 95 mph without the help of a launch (more than any other coaster is capable of). This coaster is located in Nagashima Spa Land in Japan.
